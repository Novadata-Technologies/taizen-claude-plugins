# Industry Insights Skill

Vertical market intelligence for industry-specific sales and marketing conversations.

## Purpose

Enable highly relevant, industry-specific conversations by providing vertical market intelligence, trends, challenges, and success stories.

## Supported Industries

### Healthcare
- **Key Trends**: Staffing shortages, burnout, digital transformation, value-based care
- **Regulatory Context**: HIPAA, CMS requirements, Joint Commission standards
- **Decision Makers**: CNO, CHRO, CMO, VP Clinical Operations
- **Proof Points**: Focus on patient outcomes, staff retention, compliance

### Retail
- **Key Trends**: Omnichannel, labor flexibility, seasonal workforce, customer experience
- **Regulatory Context**: Labor laws, scheduling regulations, safety standards
- **Decision Makers**: CHRO, VP Store Operations, Chief People Officer
- **Proof Points**: Focus on turnover reduction, store performance, customer satisfaction

### Financial Services
- **Key Trends**: Digital banking, regulatory compliance, fintech disruption, talent war
- **Regulatory Context**: SEC, FINRA, OCC, state banking regulations
- **Decision Makers**: CHRO, Chief Compliance Officer, Head of Talent
- **Proof Points**: Focus on compliance, risk reduction, talent retention

### Technology
- **Key Trends**: Remote work, skills gaps, rapid scaling, DEI in tech
- **Regulatory Context**: Data privacy, export controls, labor classifications
- **Decision Makers**: CHRO, VP Engineering, Chief People Officer
- **Proof Points**: Focus on velocity, innovation, employer brand

### Manufacturing
- **Key Trends**: Automation, skills gaps, safety, supply chain resilience
- **Regulatory Context**: OSHA, EPA, industry-specific certifications
- **Decision Makers**: VP HR, Plant Manager, Chief Operations Officer
- **Proof Points**: Focus on safety, uptime, workforce stability

### Hospitality
- **Key Trends**: Labor shortages, seasonal fluctuations, guest experience, tips/wages
- **Regulatory Context**: Labor laws, tipped employee regulations, safety
- **Decision Makers**: CHRO, VP Operations, Regional Directors
- **Proof Points**: Focus on turnover, guest satisfaction, operational efficiency

### Government
- **Key Trends**: Modernization, workforce aging, skills gaps, diversity mandates
- **Regulatory Context**: Federal hiring rules, union agreements, clearance requirements
- **Decision Makers**: CHRO, Agency Head, HR Director
- **Proof Points**: Focus on compliance, mission readiness, cost efficiency

## Usage

Specify the industry in `$ARGUMENTS`:

```
/taizen-gtm-skills:industry-insights healthcare
/taizen-gtm-skills:industry-insights retail
```
